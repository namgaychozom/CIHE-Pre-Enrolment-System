What is SyncMaster?

Your smarter way to plan, pre-enrol, and succeed at Crown Institute of Higher Education (CIHE). SyncMaster is an intelligent, web-based pre-enrolment system designed to help CIHE students select and manage their preferred courses before the official enrolment period begins. By submitting your pre-enrolment preferences, you help ensure a smoother, more accurate timetable and improved academic planning.

Why use SyncMaster?

  • Plan Ahead: Secure your preferred courses and avoid clashes in your schedule.
  • Smart Scheduling: Helps CIHE create optimized timetables based on real student demand.
  • Save Time: No more confusion or long enrolment queues.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple, intuitive design for a smooth user experience.

Who can use SyncMaster?

All enrolled CIHE students can log in to select and rank their desired units for the upcoming term. Academic staff and administrators also use SyncMaster to analyze demand and build efficient class schedules.
